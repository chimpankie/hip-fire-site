About The Band

Through the years, The Hip Fire have refined a unique musical approach that seamlessly moves through genres such as punk, heartland rock, prog and emo. All the while, the songs stay rooted on a lyrical expression of heartbreak, isolation, and modern existentialist fear.

The Hip Fire's upcoming album will offer the purest yet distillation of this strange and heady mix.
